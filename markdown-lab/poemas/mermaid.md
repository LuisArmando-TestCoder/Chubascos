title: "mermaid"
---
Mermaid, extending her hand;

I fell to the river,

deep in her smile

I am drowning in her soft grin.

&nbsp;

Irises reflecting the night

of a muse with a skin of glitter,

and the breeze steaming,

whirling around,

even the air,

ghostly as I,

wants to be stroked

by her holy fingers.
