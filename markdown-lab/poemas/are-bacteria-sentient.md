Are bacteria sentient?

Are insects sentient?

Are rodents sentient?

Are humans sentient?

Are you sentient?

Is God sentient?

How much of sentience?

&nbsp;

If it is measurable,

machines would be alive,

and fellow humans

with more life than others.
