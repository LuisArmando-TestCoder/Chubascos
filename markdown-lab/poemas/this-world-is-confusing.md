title: "this-world-is-confusing"
---
This world is confusing,

but I'm still here.

&nbsp;

I live in a luxurious public place,

which is the whole world;

I made up my mind

to see it that way.

&nbsp;

I'm an extravagant

human being.

&nbsp;

I'm complete,

I don't need anything,

I'm ok.
