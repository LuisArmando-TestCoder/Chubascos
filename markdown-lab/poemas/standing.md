title: "standing"
---
Standing; I'm drowning again,

testing my faith in the sink,

here underwater

I choose to live.

&nbsp;

After three falls,

after somebody has broken my jaw

after two decades of being alone,

after a death made me so numb,

I'm standing, I'm standing,

I'm standing, I'm standing.
